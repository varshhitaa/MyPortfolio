FuelEase IOS Mobile App

Project Manager | Software Development

Project Overview

As the Project Manager for the FuelEase iOS app, I led our team through the development and launch of a user-friendly mobile application aimed at helping drivers find affordable fuel prices quickly. We focused on integrating Swift UI and Cocoa frameworks to enhance the app's functionality and user interaction, which led to a notable 40% boost in user engagement.

We took advantage of Swift UI and Cocoa frameworks for their strong performance and flexibility, allowing us to offer features like real-time updates on fuel prices that users really appreciated. The response from our user base was overwhelmingly positive, showing that our app was meeting a critical need.

I oversaw the architectural planning and optimization using XCode and Figma, which helped us streamline the app’s operations. We chose Firebase to manage our database because of its scalability and reliability, which improved our app’s stability and reduced crashes by 20%. Moreover, these enhancements improved our app's overall reliability by 25%, making it a dependable tool during travel.


App Mission and Features

Our mission is to ease the challenges of finding cost-effective fuel by providing users with real-time, accurate, and accessible gas price information. The app includes features designed to enhance user satisfaction and engagement:

  • Price Transparency: Offers real-time gas price information from various sources to ensure users can find the best deals nearby.
  • Location-Based Searches: Allows users to find gas prices based on their current location or search areas, enhancing convenience.
  • Community Engagement: Enables users to contribute by reporting gas prices, which helps maintain up-to-date and accurate data.

Target Audience

FuelEase serves multiple user groups including everyday drivers, gas stations, and car insurance companies. It helps users save on fuel costs, allows gas stations to monitor competitive pricing, and supports insurance companies in promoting safe driving practices.


Technology Used

  • User Interface: Developed using Swift UI to ensure a seamless user experience.
  • Backend Logic: Handled by Swift for robust app functionality.
  • Database and APIs: Managed with Firebase, facilitating scalable and efficient data handling.
  • Development Environment: Utilized XCode for its comprehensive suite of development tools.
  • Hosting: Implemented on Firebase to provide reliable and continuous service delivery.
